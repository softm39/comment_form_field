Hidden Comment Field

Hidden Comment Field provides functionality to block out spam by adding hidden field with different values. 

Current stable version: [1.0.1](http://github.com/andrezrv/hide-this/tree/1.1.3)

## Installation

1. Unzip `hidden_comment.zip` and upload the `hidden_comment` folder to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the **"Plugins"** menu in WordPress.
3. Verify if comments are coming both while your is logged in or not logged in dashboard of your WP site
